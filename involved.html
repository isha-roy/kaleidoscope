Who are we looking for?

Kaleido welcomes students who are passionate about their chosen subjects and are willing to collaborate and work hard on their projects. Our projects are conducted online, making them accessible to students from around the world, including late middle schoolers, high schoolers, and early undergrads. We're also seeking students with prior experience to join our marketing and social media teams
