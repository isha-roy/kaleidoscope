The Kaleido Initiative

The Kaleido Initiative, in simplest terms, is a student organization that serves as a global platform for connecting students who share similar interests and passions. We provide students with the opportunity to collaborate with others who are equally passionate about their ideas and projects. At Kaleido, we're driven by genuine passion rather than labels. Our organization comprises a diverse range of clubs, each initiated and managed by students who share an unwavering commitment to their club's respective topics. We prioritize collaboration between our clubs, aiming to break free from the constraints of traditional labels. We firmly believe that students don't need to confine themselves to specific categories or titles. At Kaleido, you can explore and work at the intersection of multiple interests. In our clubs, we foster a dynamic structure with no long-term co-heads. Instead, our clubs operate through a project proposal system. Here's how it works: Students interested in leading a project create a detailed document and presentation for their club members. Club members vote on the most compelling project, and the winning project is executed. The project creator takes the lead for a designated time frame, after which the process repeats. Project ideas can range from raising awareness about mental health on social media to creating a blog on Medium or fundraising for charitable causes.
